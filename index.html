Who I Am

Passionate About Building Things That Matter

Hey! I'm Rudra Narayan Sharma, a Computer Science Engineering student at Chandigarh University (2023–2027), originally from Madhubani, Bihar. I grew up curious about how things work — and that curiosity eventually led me to code.

I specialize in frontend development and UI/UX design, with a genuine love for turning ideas into clean, responsive interfaces. Whether it's building a wellness reminder app in React or designing NLP-powered chatbots, I bring both technical skill and creative thinking to every project.

Over the past year I've completed 3 internships — at EduNet Foundation working on AI/NLP, at Motion Cut boosting frontend performance by 40%, and a governance-focused social internship at Chandigarh University. Each experience has sharpened my ability to solve real problems under real constraints.

Outside of coding, I'm drawn to design systems, open-source tools, and learning whatever is next. Right now I'm actively expanding into Next.js, TypeScript, and Docker to become a more well-rounded developer.
